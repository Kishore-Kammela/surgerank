# SurgeRank Business Analysis

## Goal
Keep product choices tied to business outcomes.

## Core Areas
- ICP and segment prioritization
- Pricing and packaging analysis
- KPI tree and launch gates
- Unit economics and margin tracking
- Churn and retention diagnostics

## Workflow
1. Start with business question (e.g., pricing change, segment focus).
2. Pull relevant metrics and assumptions.
3. Analyze impact scenarios (best/base/worst).
4. Recommend decision with success criteria.
5. Define follow-up measurement window.

## Required Outputs
- Executive summary
- Key metrics and trends
- Trade-off analysis
- Recommendation with measurable targets
- Next review date

## Guardrails
- Distinguish leading vs lagging indicators.
- Avoid vanity metrics without decision impact.
- Include confidence level and data gaps.
