# Design Partner Program

## Purpose
Define how design partners are selected, onboarded, and managed for V1 validation.

## Audience
PM, founder, operations, engineering leadership.

## Executive Summary
- Design partners are a controlled feedback channel and early validation engine.
- Program runs with clear expectations, cadence, and success criteria.

## Program Structure
- Target cohort size: 5-8 agencies.
- Onboarding timeline and milestones.
- Weekly feedback sessions with action tracking.

## Partner Selection Criteria
- Strong fit with target ICP.
- Willingness to provide structured feedback.
- Capacity to actively use product workflows.

## Success Metrics
- activation completion
- weekly active workspace usage
- report generation frequency
- conversion to paid

## Governance
- Partner issues triaged by severity and product impact.
- Scope requests routed through requirements process.
