# Personas and JTBD

## Purpose
Document primary users and jobs-to-be-done for feature and UX decisions.

## Audience
PM, design, engineering, QA, stakeholders.

## Executive Summary
- Primary user is the agency operator managing many client workspaces.
- Success depends on reducing manual reporting effort while improving decision confidence.

## Persona 1: Agency Owner/Manager
- Goals: client retention, report quality, operational visibility.
- Pain points: fragmented tools, manual reporting effort.
- JTBD: "When reviewing client performance, help me quickly identify issues and present progress clearly."

## Persona 2: SEO Analyst
- Goals: execute audits and optimizations efficiently.
- Pain points: repetitive data gathering and tool switching.
- JTBD: "When working across clients, help me prioritize high-impact actions without losing context."

## Persona 3: Stakeholder/Client Recipient (Indirect in V1)
- Goals: understand outcomes and confidence level.
- Pain points: unclear or overly technical reports.
- JTBD: "When receiving updates, help me understand progress, risk, and next actions fast."

## Mapping to V1 Features
- Command center -> Owner/Manager
- Audit + keyword modules -> Analyst
- PDF/report narratives -> Stakeholder recipient
